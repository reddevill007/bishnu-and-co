About Us

The Bishnu & Company specialises in Dished End Manufacturing. My staff and I have many years experience in the industry. We produce various types of dished ends, from 400mm to 5500mm in diameter, and thickness from 4.0mm to 25mm. We also offer the pressing and flanging of small conical sections. The Dished End Company also offer a range of single pressed or push thru Weld caps, from 219mm to 610mm, and thickness from 3.6mm to 10mm. Our emphasis is customer satisfaction and we will ensure that we supply a quality product as well as service. We have imported some excellent machinery adding to the machines built in-house, and are in the process of building more. For any further information on our standard products as well as any special pressing requirements, please do not hesitate to contact us.

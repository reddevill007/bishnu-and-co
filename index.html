About Us

Looking for a reliable sheet metal rolling and dished end manufacturing service ? Look no further than our company! Our roll-bending and curving services produce smooth radius shapes, cylindrical shapes, or cone shapes with unparalleled precision. We can handle sheet metal of various widths, from just a few millimeters up to a whopping 70 millimeters in thickness, thanks to our cutting-edge equipment that can accommodate even the largest industrial companies.

That's not all - our Dished End Company specializes in manufacturing dished ends of various types and sizes, from 100 millimeters to 4500 millimeters in diameter, and thicknesses from 2.0 millimeters to 40 millimeters. We also offer the pressing and flanging of small conical sections, as well as single pressed or push-through weld caps with diameters from 219 millimeters to 610 millimeters and thicknesses from 3.6 millimeters to 10 millimeters.

But what sets us apart is our unwavering focus on customer satisfaction. We are dedicated to providing high-quality products and services, and we use only the best equipment - including our recently imported machines - to ensure top-notch results. Our experienced staff has the expertise to handle any project, and we are constantly expanding to meet the ever-growing needs of our clients. So, if you want a quality product and unparalleled service, choose us for your sheet metal rolling and dished end manufacturing needs.
